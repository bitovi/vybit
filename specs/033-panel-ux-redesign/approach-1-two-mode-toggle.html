Approach 1 — Two-Mode Toggle

Two primary mode buttons in the header: Select and Insert. The entire panel content adapts based on the active mode. Select mode shows an action menu first, then drills into the specific editor. Insert mode shows component placement and drawing tools.

A · Select mode — action menu

After clicking an element, the user sees what they can do with it, rather than immediately landing in property editors.
